## The Vision
PodChain was conceived as a response to the inherent unreliability of autonomous AI agents. The core design goal was to strip away the "brain" from individual execution units and replace it with a deterministic, industrial-grade assembly line.

## Core Philosophical Pillars
These principles were established by the Architect and form the foundation of this repository:

1.  **The "No Brain" Pod:** A Pod is a stateless execution node. It has no memory, no autonomy, and no decision-making power. It is a pure function of its contract and its logic.
2.  **Interlocked Ignition:** The "No pass, no ignition" rule. A Pod cannot ignite unless the preceding node has cleared a deterministic Quality Gate and issued a signed receipt.
3.  **Circuit Breaking:** Failures must not propagate. If a Pod generates a non-compliant artifact, the chain is physically blocked.
4.  **Verification over Trust:** We do not "prompt" AI to be better; we "engineer" gates to verify its compliance.

## Development Note
This system was built to bridge the gap between probabilistic AI outputs and the deterministic requirements of enterprise-grade software engineering.
